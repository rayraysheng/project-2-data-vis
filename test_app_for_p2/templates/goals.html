Social Media Strategy

Project's Goals


Our project aims to understand how companies want to share their data in orther to gain more popularity over the Social Media World. We approchwd it getting first data from Instagram and than analyzing them, after a deep analyzation we draw some interesting graphs, showing us the correlation of some features that have been considered the most important for the strategy from our point of view. So, our analysis overview is primarly given from our graphs.

  1. Number of Followers vs posting frequency
    This graph is a Bubble Chart showing on the X-axis the "posting frequency" and on the Y-axis the "number of followers". Each bubble has a different size that describes the average number of likes for all the posts of each brand.
  2. Number of posts vs time period
    This graph is a Line Chart showing on the X-axis the "time of the day" and on the Y-axis the "number of posts". Each line desribes a brand, so we are going to have 10 different lines.
  3. Number of likes vs time period
    This graph is still a Line chart but this time showing on the X-axis the "time of the day" and o nthe Y-axis the "number of likes". Each line describes a bran, so we are going to have 10 different lines.
  4. Number of Hashtag vs Number of likes
    This graph is a Scatter Plot, showing on the X-axis the "Number of hashtags used" and on the Y-axis the "number of likes". This graph is going to describe separately each brand that you can choose with the drop-down menu.

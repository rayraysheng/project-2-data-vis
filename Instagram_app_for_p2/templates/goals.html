Social Media Strategy

Project's Goals


Our project aims to understand how companies want to share their data in order to gain more popularity over the Social Media World. We approched it getting first data from Instagram and than analyzing them, after a deep analyzation we draw some interesting graphs, showing us the correlation of some features that have been considered the most important for the strategy from our point of view. So, our analysis overview is primarly given from our graphs.

  1. Account Overview
    Three bar charts showing on the X-axis the brand and on the Y-axis the: (1) total number of posts; (2) total number of followers; (3) total number of accounts that each brand account follows.
  2. Number of Hashtags vs Audience Interaction
    Line chart showing on the X-axis the number of hashtags used and on the Y-axis the: (1) average likes per picture per follower; (2) average views per video per follower; (3) average number of likes per album (i.e., a post with more than one picture or video). Individual brands can be selected by accessing a drop-down menu.
  3. Post Frequency vs. Audience Interaction
    Bubble chart showing on the X-axis the posting frequency and on the Y-axis the number of likes (for pictures) or views (for videos). Each bubble represents a company and its size captures the average number of followers for that brand.
  4. Days of the Week vs Account Activities
    Three bar charts showing on the X-axis the day of the week and on the Y-axis the: (1) total number of posts; (2) total number of likes and views; (3) average number of likes and views. Individual brands can be selected by accessing a drop-down menu.

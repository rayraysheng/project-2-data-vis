Social Media Strategy

Proposal


Today the entire world is affected by the invasion of Social Media, with Instagram currently being the most popular. Unlike facebook, snapchat etc., Instagram focuses on pictures and videos. We are going to pretend to be a consulting company that provides fashion brands with sugggestions on how making their instagram presence more followed.

Most of us, browsing into Instagram, may have found ourselves picking some interesting brands to be analysed. We have been focusing on the fashion side. Every fashion brand that we chose has thousands of posts, million of followers and million of likes but we detected some differences among them.

We want to understand whether items such as hashtag count, daily sechedule, etc. affect the post popularity.
